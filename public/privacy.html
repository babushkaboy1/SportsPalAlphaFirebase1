Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Who we are. SportsPal ("SportsPal," "we," "us," "our") provides a platform to discover, join, and organize sports activities and chat with other users. Contact: sportspalapplication@gmail.com

Important: This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information. Some countries give you non-waivable rights. Nothing here limits those.

Reality check: No policy can guarantee disputes never occur, but this notice is designed to minimize risk by being precise, conservative, and transparent.

Quick Summary (plain language)

We collect: the info you provide (profile, messages, photos), device/usage data, optional location, and data from sign-in providers.

We use data to run SportsPal (profiles, discovery, activities, messaging), keep users safe (moderation, anti-abuse), and improve performance/analytics.

Profiles and activity participation are visible to others; your exact real-time location is not publicly posted without your action (we use approximate distances for discovery).

We do not sell your data and do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising under California law.

You control permissions (location, camera, notifications), and you can access, export, correct, or delete your data.

Messages are not end-to-end encrypted; we may review content for safety and to enforce rules.

Data may be processed outside your country with lawful transfer safeguards.

1) Scope and Definitions

Service: SportsPal mobile apps, sites, APIs, and related features.

Personal data / personal information: Information that identifies or can reasonably identify you.

Sensitive data: Categories defined by law (e.g., precise location, government IDs, financial data, health info, sexual life). We do not require you to provide sensitive data; do not include it in your profile or chats.

Controller: SportsPal acts as controller for your data when you use the Service.

2) Information We Collect

A. Information you provide

Account & Profile: name/username, email, password (hashed), bio, interests, favorite sports, profile photo/video, pronouns (optional).

Content: messages, photos/videos, activity titles/descriptions, comments, reactions, reports, and any attachments you upload.

Preferences: discovery radius, visibility, notifications, language, theme.

Support & Verification: inquiries, appeals, safety reports; if we request, limited ID info to verify identity for safety workflows (optional, where lawful).

B. Information collected automatically

Location (optional): approximate or precise location if you grant permission (to power discovery, distance, and safety/anti-abuse).

Device & diagnostics: device model, OS, app version, language, performance metrics, crash logs.

Identifiers: device/app identifiers, push token, IP address (security, geofencing, anti-abuse).

Usage data: feature use, screens viewed, timestamps, referrers/UTM.

C. Information from others

Sign-in providers: Apple, Google, or others you choose (e.g., name, email, auth token).

Payments (if/when offered): limited billing metadata from processors (we do not store full card numbers).

Safety/anti-abuse services: results from spam/fraud detection, block lists.

We do not intentionally collect special category/sensitive data. Do not post it.

Service delivery & core features (accounts, profiles, discovery, activities, chat, notifications).
Legal bases: Contract; Legitimate interests (smooth, safe service).

Safety, integrity, and moderation (detecting fraud/spam/abuse; processing reports; enforcing rules; protecting users).

4) Location Data

Choice: You can grant/deny location permission in system settings at any time.

Use: discovery, distance, safety/anti-abuse, basic analytics.

Display: We generally show approximate distance, not your exact coordinates.

Accuracy: GPS and network signals can be inaccurate; do not rely on the app for safety-critical navigation.

5) Messaging, Media, and Moderation

Messages are not end-to-end encrypted.

We may scan, review, or analyze content (including messages and metadata) using automated tools and/or human review to:

  • enforce our Community Rules and Terms;
  • detect spam/fraud/malware;
  • respond to user reports and legal requests.

Do not share information you consider highly confidential.

6) Sharing and Disclosures

We do not sell personal information and do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising under California law.

We share the minimum necessary with:

Other users: your public profile and content (e.g., activity participation, profile photo, username, sports). Messages are visible to chat participants.

Service providers (processors): hosting, databases, storage/CDN, push notifications, analytics/crash reporting, moderation/anti-abuse, email, build/OTA update services—each bound by contracts to process data only for us.

Change of control: in a merger, acquisition, or asset transfer, your data may transfer under this policy's protections.

Legal/safety: to comply with law or protect the rights, property, or safety of SportsPal, our users, or the public (e.g., valid court orders, emergency threats).

7) International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where we transfer personal data from the EEA/UK, we rely on lawful transfer mechanisms (e.g., EU Standard Contractual Clauses and UK addendum) and apply appropriate safeguards.

8) Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes above, including security/fraud prevention and legal compliance, then delete or anonymize it.

Typical periods (subject to change for safety/legal holds):

Account & profile: while your account is active and up to 24 months after deletion (to allow recovery and defend against abuse/fraud).

Messages & activities: while your account is active; selected records may persist longer for safety/legal reasons.

Logs/diagnostics: 12–24 months.

Reports & enforcement: retained as long as necessary to protect users and comply with law.

9) Your Choices & Controls

Permissions: manage location, camera, photos, microphone, and notifications in device settings.

Discovery & visibility: adjust profile visibility and discovery radius.

Marketing: opt out in-app or via "unsubscribe."

Blocking & reports: block users and report content anytime.

Data requests: access/export, correct, or delete your data by emailing sportspalapplication@gmail.com from your account email. We may verify identity before acting.
